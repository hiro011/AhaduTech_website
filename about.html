About ahadutech

ahadutech is a simple, fast, and fully client-side electronics store built for learning and fun.

How it works:

You can browse products on the Products page, check real-time stock availability, and add items to your cart with one click.

Your cart is saved locally in your browser using localStorage — no login, no server, no data shared.

Visit the Dashboard to view your cart, see total items and price, and remove items as needed.

Important: All cart data is saved only on this device and browser. Clear your cache or use another device — your cart will be gone.
